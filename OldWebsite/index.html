ARLearn aims to assist educational effort by illustrating study material through Augmented Reality. When completed and implemented, it will allow teachers to provide an innovative and interesting way of studying at a relatively low cost due to the ubiquity of the smartphone. We aim to integrate ARLearn into classrooms by opensourcing the project and allowing anyone to provide AR content. This will hopefully read to a more interactive and effective study experience for students. Most importantly, our project aims to turn the smartphone from a distraction, to a usefull tool.
